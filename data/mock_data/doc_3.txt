Simple and Healthy Sweet Potato Dog Treats
Looking for a tasty snack to treat your furry friend? These homemade sweet potato dog treats are easy to make and packed with natural goodness!

Ingredients
2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced

1 cup oat flour

1 egg

1 tablespoon peanut butter (make sure it’s xylitol-free)

Instructions
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Boil the sweet potatoes until soft, then mash them in a bowl.

Add oat flour, egg, and peanut butter. Mix well to form a dough.

Roll the dough into small balls and flatten them on a baking sheet.

Bake for 25-30 minutes until firm and golden brown.

Let cool before serving to your dog.

Benefits
Sweet potatoes are rich in fiber and vitamins A and C. This treat is a great snack but does not target specific health conditions like cancer. Always feed treats in moderation!
